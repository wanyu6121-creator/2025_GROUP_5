Detailed Description

动态添加Actor时携带的完整数据包。

Complete data package carried when dynamically adding an actor.

用于 CMD_ADD_ACTOR 命令,包含Actor本体及其pipeline所需的全部组件。 Used by CMD_ADD_ACTOR; contains the actor and all components required by its pipeline. 由 MainWindow 在GUI线程中准备,通过线程安全队列传递给VR线程。 Prepared by MainWindow on the GUI thread and passed to the VR thread through a thread-safe queue.
